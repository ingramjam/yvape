Choose 4-5 Questions to Discuss

1.

What do you like about using nicotine and/or marijuana? What do you dislike about using nicotine and/or marijuana?

2.

What do you know about how vaping companies, like Elf Bar, Juul, and Vuse, market their products to teens?

3.

Have any of your friends who use nicotine and/or marijuana had any health problems? Have you? If so, what? How did it affect you?

4.

What do you think the risks of using nicotine and marijuana are? How do you think using nicotine and marijuana affects long-term health?

5.

What do your friends think about using nicotine compared to marijuana? What do you think?

6.

Think about a time when you were happiest. What was going on then? What do you think you would have to do to feel like that again?

7.

What are some things that have caused you stress recently? What are a few ways you cope with stress? What might you do differently if you experience that stressor again?

8.

How do you think your life would change if you stopped using nicotine and/or marijuana? What changes might you notice in the next few weeks and what changes might you notice in the next few months?

9.

How do you think your nicotine and/or marijuana use has affected others?

10.

What are some goals you'd like achieve in the next month? The next year?
