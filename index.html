Youth Vaping Alternative Program Education

YVAPE is a FREE educational intervention program that serves as an alternative to suspension, providing phone and video coaching support to students who have been found with nicotine or marijuana at school.

Start Enrollment Learn More

Available to California public schools with students 12 years and older

How YVAPE Works

A simple, supportive process designed to help students

For a Student to be Eligible:

  • Student must attend a California public school
  • Student must be 12 years or older
  • Student was found with nicotine or marijuana at school
  • School personnel submits enrollment information
  • Parent/guardian provides verbal consent (if under 18)
  • Student provides verbal consent to participate
Enroll a Student

Provide Verbal Consent if Your Child is Younger Than 18:

  • A YVAPE coach will call you to explain the program
  • You'll have the opportunity to ask questions
  • Provide verbal consent for your child to participate
  • Your child will then be contacted to begin the program
  • All conversations are confidential and supportive
Start Process

Provide Verbal Consent to Participate:

  • A YVAPE coach will reach out to you
  • Learn about the program and what to expect
  • Provide your consent to participate
  • Watch educational videos at your own pace
  • Schedule coaching sessions that work for you
  • Complete the program and receive a certificate

*While YVAPE is not a cessation program, students who want help quitting tobacco can be enrolled in Kick It California's tobacco cessation services.

Benefits of YVAPE

Comprehensive support at no cost to schools

Education

Students learn about nicotine, marijuana, health, stress, motivation and responsibility through engaging videos and coaching.

Flexible Scheduling

Students can schedule their voice or video appointments for a time that works for them, making it easy to fit into their schedule.

Reports

Confidential aggregate reports are provided to schools each semester, helping track program effectiveness.

100% Free

YVAPE is a free service for California public schools with students 12 years and older. No cost to schools or families.
