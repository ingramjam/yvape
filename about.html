YVAPE is Operated by Kick It California

YVAPE is operated by Kick It California and is funded by the California Department of Education. The program is directed by Shu-Hong Zhu, PhD, professor of Family Medicine & Public Health at UC San Diego.

Who We Are

The coaches are here to help. They have years of experience with talking with people who vape and lots of enthusiasm for working with teens.

Our Mission

Educate students about nicotine and marijuana use, support a decrease in recidivism and suspension rates, build a positive bond between Kick It California and students, and provide resources for students who want to reduce or quit nicotine and marijuana use.

Our Values

Commitment to student well-being, respect, trust, empowerment, education, empathy, communication, and inclusivity.

Kick It California & YVAPE

The original helpline began in 1992 for smoking cessation, and has since been staffed by caring, trained professionals. Clinical trials have shown that the program's approach can double a smoker's chance of quitting for good. In 2019, we launched a vaping-specific coaching protocol.

YVAPE is a new program but it is run by the same dedicated staff from Kick It California who apply strategies gained from many years of experience helping teens stop using tobacco products.

If you want help with quitting vaping once you complete YVAPE, check out our vaping resources.
