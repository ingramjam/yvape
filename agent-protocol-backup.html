Optional transition:

1. "When it comes to nicotine/marijuana, how do you see it fitting in your life now?"

Code Only

2. "On a scale of 0-10, how important is it for you to (avoid/cut down) nicotine/marijuana in the future?"

Optional Directions
  • Tell me why you say that?
  • Ask student why it's a X and not a lower number
  • Probe for more responses. Most students will be able to give 3 reasons
  • Can remind students of troubles it caused them/downsides

3. "On a scale of 0-10 how confident are you that you could avoid nicotine/marijuana if you decided to?"

Optional Directions
  • Ask student why it's a X and not a lower number
  • What would it take to raise your confidence level by 1 number?
  • Offer resources as needed
